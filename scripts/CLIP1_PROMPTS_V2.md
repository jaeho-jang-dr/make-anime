## Clip 1-1: 대전역 광장 (8초)

**Prompt:**
```
Studio Ghibli anime style, warm summer morning.

Daejeon Station in South Korea - modern glass building with large Korean text sign "대전역" (Daejeon Station) clearly visible. Korean family walking toward entrance - young mother in summer dress, father carrying bags, 8-year-old boy Hyewan with short black hair wearing striped t-shirt, holding mom's hand.

The boy looks up at the station excitedly. Korean atmosphere, Korean people in background. Bright sunlight, blue sky with white clouds.

Korean dialogue - Mom says: "혜완아, 할머니 할아버지 보러 가는 거 신나니?"
Boy replies happily: "네! 빨리 가고 싶어요!"

Camera slowly pans showing the beautiful Korean train station exterior.
```

## Clip 1-2: 승강장에서 기다리기 (8초)

**Prompt:**
```
Studio Ghibli anime style, continuing from previous scene.

Inside Daejeon KTX station platform. Korean signs showing "밀양행" (To Miryang) and platform numbers in Korean. Modern clean platform with safety doors.

The 8-year-old Korean boy stands excitedly looking down the tracks, standing on tiptoes. Mother in summer dress smiles beside him, father checks phone. Electronic display board shows Korean text "KTX 밀양행 곧 도착" (KTX to Miryang arriving soon).

Korean dialogue - Boy asks eagerly: "엄마, 기차 언제 와요?"
Mom answers: "곧 온대. 저기 봐봐!"

Camera focuses on the boy's anticipating face, then pans to show the tracks.
```

## Clip 1-3: KTX 도착 (8초)

**Prompt:**
```
Studio Ghibli anime style, continuing scene.

White and blue KTX high-speed train (Korean Train eXpress) arriving at Daejeon station platform. Sleek aerodynamic nose design, Korean flag logo on side, "KTX" written on the train. Wind blows as train smoothly stops.

The boy's eyes go wide with wonder, pointing at the train. His short black hair moves in the wind. Parents smile at his excited reaction. Platform safety doors preparing to open.

Korean dialogue - Boy shouts excitedly: "와! KTX다! 진짜 크다!"
Dad says: "저거 타고 밀양 가는 거야!"

Dynamic camera following the train arriving, then focusing on the amazed boy.
```

## Clip 1-4: 기차 문 열리고 탑승 (8초)

**Prompt:**
```
Studio Ghibli anime style, continuous action.

KTX train doors sliding open smoothly, revealing clean modern interior with blue seats. Platform safety doors also open. The excited 8-year-old boy rushes toward the train door, looking back at parents with biggest smile.

Mother follows holding his hand saying "천천히!", father carries luggage behind them. Other Korean passengers also boarding. Korean announcement playing. Train interior visible - neat rows of comfortable seats, overhead luggage racks.

Korean dialogue - Boy says eagerly: "빨리 타요! 빨리!"
Mom says gently: "천천히, 혜완아. 뛰면 안 돼."

Camera follows the family stepping into the train through the open doors.
```

## Clip 1-5: 창가 자리에 앉기 (8초)

**Prompt:**
```
Studio Ghibli anime style, interior scene.

Inside the KTX train car - modern clean interior with blue fabric seats, large windows, soft lighting, overhead compartments. The boy runs down the aisle and claims a window seat, pressing his face against the large window excitedly.

Parents settle into seats beside him, putting bags in overhead rack. Mom sits next to the boy, dad across the aisle. The boy's reflection visible in the window glass. Other Korean passengers seated quietly.

Korean dialogue - Boy announces proudly: "나 창가! 창가 자리다!"
Dad chuckles: "그래, 잘했어. 밖에 다 보이겠다."

Camera shows the cozy family seated together, focusing on the happy boy at the window.
```

## Clip 1-6: 기차 출발 (8초)

**Prompt:**
```
Studio Ghibli anime style, departure moment.

KTX train begins to move smoothly. View from inside - Daejeon station platform slowly sliding away through the large window. Korean signs "대전역" visible outside getting smaller. Gentle acceleration feeling.

The boy watches with sparkling eyes, waving at the platform. Mom puts her arm around him lovingly. Soft vibration of the moving train. Afternoon sunlight streaming through window.

Korean dialogue - Boy waves and says: "대전 안녕! 우리 간다!"
Mom says warmly: "밀양까지 1시간이야. 할머니가 기다리고 계셔."

Camera captures the magical moment of departure, the station disappearing in the distance.
```

**Prompt:**

Studio Ghibli anime style, train journey scene.

KTX train at full speed. View through large window - beautiful Korean countryside rushing by. Green rice paddies (논), distant blue mountains, fluffy white clouds, small Korean villages. Motion blur showing high speed.

The boy's amazed face reflected in window glass, eyes wide with wonder. He points at the scenery excitedly. Mom and dad watch him with warm loving smiles. Peaceful train interior, gentle humming sound.

## Flow 제작 팁

### 자연스러운 연결을 위해:
1. 각 클립 시작에 **"continuing from previous scene"** 추가
2. 캐릭터 외모 일관성 유지 (boy with short black hair, striped shirt)
3. 같은 장소/상황 유지

### Extend 사용 시:
- 이전 클립의 마지막 장면을 언급
- "continuous action", "same characters" 명시

### 안 되면:
- 각 클립을 개별 생성 후 Scene Builder에서 연결
- Jump to 대신 개별 생성이 더 일관성 있을 수 있음
